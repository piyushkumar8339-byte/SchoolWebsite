About Us

Pragyan Public School, Jamuniya, run by Pragyan Educational Trust, focuses on providing moral, ethical, and high-quality education. Our vision is to prepare children for influential roles in society and to foster confident, dynamic, and sensitive individuals.

Academics

  • CBSE-based curriculum
  • Equal emphasis on curricular & co-curricular activities
  • Child-centered, value-based teaching methods
  • Extra/remedial classes (Zero Period)

Admissions

Eligibility: LKG - 4 years, UKG - 5 years as on 31st March. For higher classes, age as per norms.

Process: Admission through written test & personality test. For Nursery, only personality test with parents present.

Documents Required:

  • Birth Certificate
  • Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Medical Fitness Certificate + Blood Group Report
  • 3 Passport Photos
  • Student & Parent Aadhaar
